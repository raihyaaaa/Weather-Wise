Enrollment Requirements

Account Creation



  • Prospective learners must create an account on the Weather Wise Academy platform. This account will serve as the portal for course enrollment, progress tracking, and access to course materials.

Device and Internet Access



  • As the courses are delivered online, learners need access to a reliable internet connection and a device (computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone) that meets the minimum technical requirements outlined by Weather Wise Academy.


    • Additional Requirements:


    Payment

  • Some courses may require payment for enrollment. Learners will need to complete the payment process using the secure online payment gateway provided on the platform. Weather Wise Academy may offer various payment options, including credit/debit cards or other accepted methods.

    • Prerequisites (if applicable)

  • Certain advanced courses may have prerequisites. Learners must meet these requirements to ensure they have the foundational knowledge necessary for successful completion of the course.

    • Agreement to Terms and Conditions:

  • Before enrolling in any course, learners will be required to review and agree to the terms and conditions of Weather Wise Academy. This includes adherence to the code of conduct, privacy policies, and any other relevant guidelines.

    • Confirmation of Enrollment

  • Once all requirements are met, learners will receive a confirmation of enrollment, along with details on accessing the course materials, participating in discussions, and engaging with the learning community.

    • Technical Proficiency Assessment

  • To ensure a smooth learning experience, learners may need to complete a brief technical proficiency assessment. This may include basic computer literacy and familiarity with online learning tools.

    • Commitment to Learning

  • Weather Wise Academy values learners who are committed to the educational journey. Prospective students may be asked to provide a brief statement expressing their commitment to learning and how the course aligns with their goals.
